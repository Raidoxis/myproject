4PDA Russian forum

Since its launch in 2005 4pda.ru has become one of the most popular and trustworthy sites about smartphones and mobile devices. As of September 2013, 4PDA ranks second among Russian Internet websites in Computers category. It has been in the top three for two years running. 4PDA is the most popular website about smartphones running under Android OS, Windows Phone 7, and Windows Mobile in Russia and Ukraine. 4PDA is one of the most popular websites about GPS-tracking and corresponding devices in Russia. 4PDA has earned its reputation as a reliable source of up-to-date information about PDAs, smartphones, e-book readers, tablet computers, navigators and other mobile devices.
